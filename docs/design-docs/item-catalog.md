# Item Catalog

A reference list of all player-available equipment, consumables, and curses currently in the game.

---

## Equipment

- Weapon:
  - Flint Spear (8g) — +5 damage
  - Poison-Tipped Dirk (7g) — +2 damage, + Poison 2
  - Flint Spear (10g) — +10 damage if hand is lower than 20 
  - Bronze Saif (25g) — +14 flat damage
  - Cavalry Saber (26g) — +12 flat damage + Bleed 2
  - Bronze Kilij (29g) — +16 flat damage
  - Iron Scimitar (45g) — +28 flat damage + Bleed 1
  - Iron Javelin (46g) — +22 flat damage + Bleed 5
  - Iron Mace (44g) — +38 damage if hand contains a spade
  - Viper's Bite (77g) — +30 flat damage + Poison 10

- Helm:
  - Cloth Helm (20g) — 30% less damage on bust
  - Bronze Helm (45g) — 50% less damage on bust
  - Iron Helm (80g) — 80% less damage on bust

- Armor:
  - Cloth Armor (25g) — 20% less incoming damage
  - Bronze Armor (55g) — 40% less incoming damage
  - Iron Armor (90g) — 60% less incoming damage

- Boots:
  - Cloth Boots (20g) — 10% dodge chance
  - Bronze Boots (50g) — 25% dodge chance
  - Iron Boots (85g) — 40% dodge chance

- Trinket:
  - Cloth Trinket (15g) — +10 gold per battle
  - Bronze Trinket (40g) — 25% less damage from a randomly chosen suit each battle
  - Iron Trinket (75g) — Bust counts as a score of 10 (negates busting)

## Consumables

- Health Potion (10g) — Restores 5 HP (instant)
- Damage Potion (15g) — Deals 5 damage to the enemy (instant)
- Strength Potion (20g) — +30% damage for 1 hand
- Poison Potion (20g) — Deals 3 damage/hand to the enemy for 3 hands

---

## Curses

Curses are permanent debuffs applied to the player after defeating a boss at the Genie encounter. They persist for the remainder of the run.

- Night Fang Curse (Stage 1 — Ancient Strix) — Enemies deal +5 damage when they hit blackjack
- Warden Curse (Stage 2 — Djinn Warden) — Take 3 damage at the start of each hand
- Crimson Curse (Stage 3 — Crimson Sultan) — Ties (pushes) favor the dealer; player loses instead of a draw
