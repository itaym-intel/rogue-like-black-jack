# Effect Catalog

A reference list of all player, combatant, and equipment effects in the game.

---

## Damage Effects

- Bleed:
  - Bleed — Deals X damage per hand for X hands. Going down to X-1 after every hand (lasts for X hands). Applied by weapons or abilities. Stacks additively (two applications of Bleed 2 = 4 damage/hand for 4 hands).

- Poison:
  - Poison — Deals X damage per hand for N hands. Applied by the Poison Potion or enemy abilities. Stacks additively.

## Stat Effects

- Strength:
  - Strength — Increases outgoing damage by X% for N hands. Applied by the Strength Potion or certain equipment.

- Weak:
  - Weak — Reduces outgoing damage by X% for N hands. Can be applied to either the player or enemy.

## Defensive Effects

- Dodge:
  - Dodge — X% chance to negate all incoming damage from a hand. Granted by boots equipment.

- Armor:
  - Damage Reduction — Reduces all incoming damage by X%. Granted by armor equipment. Applies after all other modifiers.

- Bust Guard:
  - Bust Guard — Reduces damage taken when the player busts by X%. Granted by helm equipment.

## Persistent Effects (Curses)

- Night Fang:
  - Night Fang — Enemies deal +5 damage when they hit blackjack. Applied by the Stage 1 boss curse.

- Warden:
  - Warden — Player takes 3 damage at the start of each hand. Applied by the Stage 2 boss curse.

- Crimson:
  - Crimson — Ties (pushes) resolve as dealer wins. Applied by the Stage 3 boss curse.

## Suit Effects

- Suit Resistance:
  - Suit Resistance — Player takes X% less damage from hands where the dealer's scoring cards belong to a chosen suit. Granted by the Bronze Trinket; suit is randomized each battle.

## Gold Effects

- Gold Gain:
  - Gold Bonus — Player earns +X gold at the end of each battle. Granted by the Cloth Trinket.
